# Cognitive Level of Analysis

The cognitive level of analysis focuses on mental processes such as perception, memory, language, and
decision making. It operates on the principle that the mind can be studied scientifically by inferring
internal mental states from observable behaviour, and that cognitive processes are influenced by social
and cultural factors.

- [Memory Models](./cognitive/memory-models) -- the multi-store model, working memory model,

- [Thinking and Decision Making](./cognitive/thinking-and-decision-making) -- heuristics, biases,

- [Language and Thought](./cognitive/language-and-thought) -- the Sapir-Whorf hypothesis, bilingualism,
