# Freshwater Issues

This section covers the IB Geography compulsory core extension on freshwater issues. It examines the
hydrological cycle, the characteristics and functioning of drainage basins, the causes and consequences
of water scarcity, and the strategies used to manage freshwater resources and flood risks.

## Topics

- [Drainage Basins and Hydrology](./freshwater/drainage-basins-and-hydrology) -- the hydrological cycle,
  drainage basin systems, and river processes.
- [Water Scarcity and Management](./freshwater/water-scarcity-and-management) -- causes of water
  scarcity, supply and demand management strategies.
- [Flood Management](./freshwater/flood-management) -- flood causes, impacts, and management
  approaches including hard and soft engineering.
