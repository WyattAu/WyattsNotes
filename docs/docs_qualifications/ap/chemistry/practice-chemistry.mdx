### Thermodynamics

<PracticeProblem question="The enthalpy of combustion of methane is -890 kJ/mol. ### Kinetics and Equilibrium

<PracticeProblem question="A catalyst increases the rate of a chemical reaction by:" options={[ 'Providing an alternative reaction pathway with a lower activation energy', 'Increasing the temperature of the reaction system', 'Shifting the equilibrium toward products', 'Increasing the activation energy of the reverse reaction', ]} correctAnswer={0} explanation="A catalyst provides an alternative reaction mechanism with a lower activation energy barrier.
