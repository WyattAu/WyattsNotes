# DSE Economics

Complete revision notes for HKDSE Economics, covering both microeconomics and macroeconomics with worked examples, data-response practice, and exam-style problems. Content follows the HKEAA curriculum structure.

- **[Demand, Supply, and Markets](./economics-demand-supply-markets)** -- Demand and supply analysis, market equilibrium, elasticity, government intervention, and market failure
- **[Market Failure and Government Intervention](./market-failure-and-government-intervention)** -- Externalities, public goods, information asymmetry, taxation, and regulation
- **[Market Structure and Theory of the Firm](./market-structure-and-theory-of-the-firm)** -- Perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly, monopolistic competition, and profit maximisation

## Macroeconomics

- **[Fiscal and Monetary Policy](./fiscal-and-monetary-policy)** -- Government spending, taxation, money supply, interest rates, inflation, and unemployment
- **[Money and Banking](./money-and-banking)** -- Functions of money, banking system, money creation, central bank roles, and monetary policy transmission

## International Economics

- **[International Trade and Finance](./international-trade-and-finance)** -- Comparative advantage, trade policies, balance of payments, exchange rates, and trading blocs
