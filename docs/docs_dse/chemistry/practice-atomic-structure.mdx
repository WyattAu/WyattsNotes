### Periodic Trends

### Ionic Bonding

Higher ionic charge and smaller ionic radii produce stronger ionic bonds and higher melting points."

      ],
      correct: 1,
      explanation: "Ionic compounds conduct electricity only when their ions are free to move. In the solid state, ions are fixed in the lattice. When molten or dissolved in water, ions become mobile and can carry charge. This is a key distinguishing property of ionic vs metallic bonding."

### Covalent and Metallic Bonding

### Intermolecular Forces
