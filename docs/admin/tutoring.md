## Worked Examples

### Example 1: Booking an IB Mathematics HL Session
**Problem:** A student needs help with integration by parts for an upcoming IB Math HL exam.
**Solution:** Navigate to the booking page, select "IB Mathematics (HL)" as the subject, choose a convenient time slot, and note the specific topic ("integration by parts and reduction formulae") in the booking notes so the tutor can prepare targeted materials.

### Example 2: Choosing Between Individual and Group Sessions
**Problem:** Two students in the same class want help with DSE Physics mechanics.
**Solution:** Book a group session (2-3 students) for a lower per-student rate. Both students benefit from shared problem-solving and discussion. If the students are at different levels or studying different topics, book individual sessions instead.

## Common Pitfalls

- **Not specifying the topic when booking:** Tutors prepare differently for calculus, mechanics, or probability problems. Always state the specific topic or syllabus area in the booking notes.
- **Assuming all tutors cover all boards:** Tutors specialise in specific qualification boards (IB, A-Level, DSE). Confirm the tutor's board expertise before booking.
- **Waiting until the last minute:** Booking 24-48 hours in advance gives the tutor time to prepare. Last-minute bookings limit preparation quality.
