    'Only cation vacancies',
    'Only anion vacancies',
  ]}
  correctAnswer={0}
  explanation"Schottky defect: a cation-anion vacancy pair. Both a cation and an adjacent anion are missing from their lattice sites. This maintains charge neutrality (equal numbers of positive and negative charge removed) but creates lattice vacancies that increase entropy. Frenkel defect: an ion moves from a lattice site to an interstitial site, creating a vacancy-interstitial pair. Both increase entropy at $T > 0$."
