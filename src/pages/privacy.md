# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 4, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Wyatt's Notes ("we", "us", "our") handles your information when
you visit [wyattsnotes.wyattau.com](https://wyattsnotes.wyattau.com).

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, your browser may automatically send certain information to our hosting
provider (GitHub Pages). This may include:

- **IP address** (anonymized by Cloudflare)
- **Browser type and version**
- **Operating system**
- **Referring URL**
- **Pages visited and time spent**

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
- We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing
- We do not use advertising networks or third-party trackers

## Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand site usage. If analytics are enabled, they
will comply with the following principles:

- **No personal data collection** — analytics data is aggregated and anonymized
- **No cookies required** — analytics work without setting tracking cookies
- **No data sharing** — analytics data is not shared with advertisers
- **GDPR compliant** — no consent banners needed because no personal data is collected

If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect the specific service
used.

## External Services

### GitHub Pages

Our site is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). GitHub's privacy policy applies to
data processed by their infrastructure:
[https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)

### Cloudflare

Our site uses [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/) for DNS and CDN services. Cloudflare may
collect connection data as described in their privacy policy:
[https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

### Search

Site search is performed entirely in your browser using a client-side index. Your search queries are
**never sent to any server**.

## Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites (e.g., Godbolt Compiler Explorer, official
documentation). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We
encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information transmitted through our site, including:

- HTTPS encryption for all connections (enforced by Cloudflare)
- No sensitive data is stored on our servers
- The site is a static site — there is no database or server-side processing of user data

## Children's Privacy

Our site is an educational resource and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

- **Right to access** — request information about what data we hold about you
- **Right to deletion** — request deletion of any personal data
- **Right to object** — object to processing of your data

Since we do not collect personal identifying information, these rights are largely academic, but we
respect them fully.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an
updated "Last updated" date.
